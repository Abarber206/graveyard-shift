GRAVEYARD SHIFT

A VR zombie wave shooter. Put on your Quest, open this page in the headset browser, and press Enter VR below.

Grip: grab / holster  •  Trigger: shoot / flashlight  •  Left stick: move  •  Right stick: snap turn
A / X: reload  •  Y: stationary mode on/off  •  B: laser sight

No headset? Desktop preview: click to look, WASD move, Left-click shoot, F flashlight, R reload.
